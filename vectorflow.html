The tools that make it work

Priority Finder Questions

Five questions to surface the workflows worth examining. Helps you cut through the noise and find where AI can have real impact.

Used for: Surface & Select

SIMPLE Framework

A structure for breaking any workflow into auditable tasks:

  • Signal - What triggers the work?
  • Inputs - What do you gather?
  • Make - What do you do with it?
  • Pick - What decisions get made?
  • Land - What do you deliver?
  • Examine - How do you know it's right?

Used for: Decompose

HAS Scale

Human Automation Scale - rates how much human involvement a task needs, from H1 (fully automated) to H5 (human only).

Used for: Assess Desire

Opportunity Scorecards

For each opportunity worth pursuing:

  • Benefit: Time saved + people happiness
  • Implementation: Easy / Medium / Hard
  • Recommendation: Build now, explore further, or park it

Used for: Prioritise
