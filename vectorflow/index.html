You've got hundreds of workflows - the processes your teams repeat every day - and thousands of tasks within them. Where do you even start?

From discovery to deployment

Surface & Select

Find the workflows worth examining

You lead

You have hundreds of processes your teams repeat daily. Using our Priority Finder Questions, we help you cut through the noise and identify which workflows have the highest potential for AI impact.

Tool: Priority Finder Questions
Outcome: A shortlist of 3-5 high-potential workflows to explore further

Decompose

Break workflows into tasks you can actually assess

You lead

You take each priority workflow and break it into discrete tasks using our SIMPLE framework. Signal, Inputs, Make, Pick, Land, Examine - six lenses that reveal exactly what's happening and where AI might help.

Tool: SIMPLE Framework
Outcome: A task-level map of each workflow, ready for assessment

Assess (Desire)

Find out what your people actually want

You lead

You survey the people who do the work. Using our HAS Scale, they rate each task from H1 (fully automate this) to H5 (keep this human). No assumptions - real data on what your teams want AI to touch.

Tool: HAS Scale (Human Automation Scale)
Outcome: Desire ratings for every task, straight from the people who do them

Assess (Feasibility)

We tell you what AI can actually do today

We lead

You send us your task maps and desire ratings. We assess every task against current AI capabilities - what's possible now, what's emerging, and what's still out of reach. No hype, just honest assessment.

Outcome: Feasibility ratings for every task, grounded in real-world AI capability

Prioritise

Where desire meets feasibility, you find your opportunities

Together

We bring together what your people want and what AI can do. The overlap reveals your real opportunities - ranked by benefit and effort, with clear recommendations on what to build first.

Tool: Opportunity Scorecards
Outcome: Opportunity Scorecards ranking each opportunity by benefit vs implementation effort
Discovery complete. Now we build.

Build

Turn the best opportunities into working solutions

We lead

We design and build AI solutions for your priority opportunities. Solution design, development, testing, deployment support - whatever's needed to get it live and working.

Outcome: Working AI solutions ready for your teams to use

Embed

Make it stick - adoption is where value lives

Together

A solution nobody uses is worthless. We help you land the change - training, adoption support, feedback loops - until it's business as usual. You keep the capability; we're not here to create dependency.

Outcome: Adopted solutions delivering real value, and a team who can repeat the process

The tools that make it work

Priority Finder Questions

Five questions to surface the workflows worth examining. Helps you cut through the noise and find where AI can have real impact.

Used for: Surface & Select

SIMPLE Framework

A structure for breaking any workflow into auditable tasks:

  • Signal - What triggers the work?
  • Inputs - What do you gather?
  • Make - What do you do with it?
  • Pick - What decisions get made?
  • Land - What do you deliver?
  • Examine - How do you know it's right?

Used for: Decompose

HAS Scale

Human Automation Scale - rates how much human involvement a task needs, from H1 (fully automated) to H5 (human only).

Used for: Assess Desire

Opportunity Scorecards

For each opportunity worth pursuing:

  • Benefit: Time saved + people happiness
  • Implementation: Easy / Medium / Hard
  • Recommendation: Build now, explore further, or park it

Used for: Prioritise
