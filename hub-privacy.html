Hub Privacy Notice

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This notice explains how AgentFlow Ltd handles personal data for invited users of Pitch Hub and Client Hub.

Who We Are

AgentFlow Ltd operates AgentFlow hub services. For privacy questions, contact admin@goagentflow.com.

Data We Process

  • Identity and contact data: name, work email, organisation details where provided.
  • Access and security data: verification requests, access tokens, device verification records, IP/log metadata, and security events.
  • Hub usage data: login events, page interactions, document/video/proposal engagement events, message activity metadata.
  • Hub content data: documents, messages, and updates uploaded/shared in a hub by authorised users.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and secure access to hub content and collaboration features.
  • Operate notifications and communication workflows between staff and invited hub users.
  • Provide usage visibility to authorised staff in the relevant hub/account context.
  • Prevent abuse, investigate incidents, and maintain service reliability and auditability.
  • Meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

Legal Bases (B2B Context)

Depending on context, processing is based on contract performance with business customers, legitimate interests in operating and securing a B2B platform, and legal obligations. For non-essential analytics technologies, consent is requested where required.

Who We Share Data With

We use processors/subprocessors for infrastructure, storage, authentication, and transactional messaging. See our Hub Subprocessors and Transfer Summary.

International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside your jurisdiction, we use applicable legal safeguards and contractual mechanisms.

Retention (Summary)

Data Category Typical Retention
Access recovery and verification artifacts Short-lived; deleted/expired according to operational retention policy.
Hub events and audit logs Retained for security and service operations for defined policy windows.
Hub content and collaboration records Retained during customer engagement and according to contractual/legal requirements.

Your Rights

Where applicable, individuals may request access, correction, deletion, objection, or restriction. We will handle requests in line with applicable law and our customer obligations.

Contact and Complaints

Contact us at admin@goagentflow.com. If unresolved, you may also contact your local data protection authority where applicable.
