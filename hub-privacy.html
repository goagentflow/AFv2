Hub Privacy Notice

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This notice explains how AgentFlow handles personal data for Pitch Hub and Client Hub users.

What We Collect

  • Contact data: name, work email, company name where provided.
  • Access data: verification requests, sign-in events, token/device records, IP and technical logs.
  • Hub activity: hub views, document/video/proposal interactions, and portal message metadata.
  • Hub content data: files and messages that are uploaded or shared in a hub.

How We Use Data

  • Provide secure access to hubs and shared materials.
  • Operate hub features (documents, updates, messaging, notifications).
  • Maintain platform security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot incidents.
  • Provide engagement visibility to authorised staff users.

Legal Basis (B2B Context)

We generally process hub user data to perform our contract with business customers, and for legitimate interests in operating and securing a B2B service. Where consent is required for non-essential cookies or tracking, we request it separately.

Who We Share With

We use service providers to host and operate the service, including infrastructure, authentication, email delivery, storage, and analytics tooling. We require contractual safeguards with processors.

International Transfers

Where data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate contractual and legal transfer safeguards.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for service delivery, security, legal obligations, and legitimate operational records, then delete or anonymise it.

Your Rights and Contact

To request access, correction, deletion, or raise a privacy concern, email admin@goagentflow.com.
